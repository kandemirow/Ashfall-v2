      HOW TO PLAY

      Enemies swarm endlessly. Your attacks fire automatically — you only move. Collect XP shards, level up, pick upgrades, and combine a maxed weapon with the right passive to evolve it from a boss chest.

      CONTROLS

      WASD / Arrows — move
      Mouse / tap — menu & upgrade picks
      Esc — release controls
      Mobile: drag the on-screen stick.

      🤝 FIGHT TOGETHER

      Staying close to teammates grants a Co-op damage buff (up to +24% with a full squad). Revive downed allies by standing near them. Grouping up is stronger — and safer — than scattering.
